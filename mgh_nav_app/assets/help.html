How To Use?

  1. 1. Select your starting position. You can do that by either scanning a Barcode near you or manually searching for your position.
    • To scan a barcode, just press the scan button
    • To enter manually, press the list button
  2. Enter your desired ending location by pressing the list button
  3. select the Floor
  4. Select the Room
    • you can search for the room by entering the room in the search field
    • you may press the microphone on your keyboard to search by saying the room number*
  5. Press the Go button to start your route
  6. Hold the phone in the orientation that the map appears in, horizontal or vertical.
  7. Start your route
  8. Press the next button when you arrive to your next point
  9. The picture in the top center tells which direction to take next
  10. You can go to a previous point by pressing the previous button


Additional Features

  • If you get lost, you can shake the phone or select Menu then Lost? and enter your current location and it will recalculate.
  • In the main screen you could press the menu key then settings and edit your prefrences
  • In the main screen you could press the menu key then the help button to call someone from MGH


*might not work with all phones
